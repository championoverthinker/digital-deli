# Why here?

> Published on Jun 7, 2025

Trying to somewhat follow the final part of this timeless advice by [Mary Oliver](https://readalittlepoetry.com/2014/09/10/sometimes-by-mary-oliver/):

Pay Attention.  
Be Astonished.  
Tell about it.  

The first two pointers here will always be a work in progress, so I'll try to tell about some of that progress here using words that appear in my consciousness from time to time.
